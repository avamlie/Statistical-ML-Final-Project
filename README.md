## Introduction

One of the goals of stock investors is to discover latent trading patterns in stock market to forecast future price trends and maximize profits. However, the prediction of stock prices using time series is a very challenging task, since they are affected by uncertain political-economic factors in the real world and are also non-stationary and non-linear.
